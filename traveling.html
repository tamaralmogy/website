Traveling

Italy

In September, I embarked on a three-week Italian escapade with two dear friends, starting in Milan for the F1 Grand Prix at Monza before journeying to the idyllic shores of Lake Como. Our route then led us southwest to the enchanting villages of Cinque Terre, followed by a venture eastward through the sun-kissed vineyards of Tuscany, with Florence as a captivating highlight.
Our whirlwind tour concluded back in Milan, with the timeless beauty of Italy savored alongside copious amounts of gelato.

New York

In April 2022, accompanied by my dad and sister, I ventured to New York City, where we indulged in a diverse range of experiences. From witnessing the captivating performance of "Chicago" featuring Pamela Anderson to watching a thrilling NBA game, every moment was filled with excitement. Strolling through the iconic Central Park, exploring museums, we soaked in the city's vibrant atmosphere and timeless charm.

India

In August 2019, I embarked on a two-and-a-half-month journey to India and Sri Lanka, traversing the majestic landscapes and vibrant cultures of both countries. In India, I explored the northern regions, from the lofty heights of Leh to the tranquil allure of Kashmir, the spiritual ambiance of Dharamsala, and the rugged expanses of Spiti Valley. Immersing myself in India's rich tapestry of people and traditions was a profound experience, fostering deep connections and a newfound appreciation for its diversity. Sri Lanka, with its pristine beaches and legendary surf breaks, provided a stark yet delightful contrast, offering endless days of thrilling wave rides and evenings lulled by the rhythmic oceanic embrace. Both countries left an indelible mark on my soul, igniting a passion for exploration and a lasting love for their captivating beauty and uniqueness.
